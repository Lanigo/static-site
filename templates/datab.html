Database Management Systems

DBMS are the higher-level software, working with lower-level APIs. They can be divided into Relational Model(SQL) and Model-less (NoSQL) Approach. SQL is table-based and NoSQL is document(key-value pair) based. I have resources here for MySQL(SQL) and MongoDB(NoSQL). It's up to you to decide which one fits your projects best.

MySQL

MySQL is a free relational database. It has a very strict nature of forming and handling data.

MongoDB

NoSQL databases, by using an unstructured (or structured-on-the-go) approach, aim to be less strict about how data is stored. They offer many different types of ways to keep and work with the data.
